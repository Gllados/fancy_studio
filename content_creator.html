about us

Meet the FancyStudio team. We are a dedicated group of gamers, who strive to deliver the best possible gaming experiences. At our core lies a belief that games should be played everywhere and at any time.

The digital world allowed us to fulfill our dream: spend our days surrounded by talented people creating products that allow people around the world to enjoy their days just that tiny bit more!

Sounds like something you might enjoy? We’re constantly searching for our next amazing team member – just know that we’re headquartered in Lviv, Ukraine!

Career

Content Creator
Location: Lviv, Ukraine

About the position:

At Fanatee, the Content Team is the heart and soul of our products. As a Content Creator for CodyCross, your work will have direct impact on the player's experience and will influence users all aroound the world.

We are looking for a talented person for the Content Creator role. The role is a freelancer position, which allows you to work remotely. You will be creating the questions and answers that are the core part of CodyCross. Your work will influence the experience of players in your language wherever they are.

We are looking for a detail-oriented and analytical person who is passionate about delivering high quality work on time. You must be able to work, follow deadlines and report your status independently. Also, you must speak fluent English and be a native speaker of your local language.

Qualifications:

  • Major in language or communications- related fields (Linguistics, Communication, Journalism)
  • Speak fluent English as second language
  • Native speaker of Japanese
  • Experience in content production in a variety of subjects and areas of knowledge
  • Analytical and detail-oriented
  • Quality and user-oriented
  • Enthusiasm or previous experience with word/trivia games is a big plus

Requirements:

  • You must be a user-oriented person who delivers high quality work on time.
  • You must be able to work, follow deadlines and report your status independently and remotely.
  • You must be able to produce and deliver factually correct content that is original, interesting and fun in a variety of subjects.
  • You must be able to organize and run your own work schedule to deliver against the deadlines agreed.
