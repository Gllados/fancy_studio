about us

Meet the FancyStudio team. We are a dedicated group of gamers, who strive to deliver the best possible gaming experiences. At our core lies a belief that games should be played everywhere and at any time.

The digital world allowed us to fulfill our dream: spend our days surrounded by talented people creating products that allow people around the world to enjoy their days just that tiny bit more!

Sounds like something you might enjoy? We’re constantly searching for our next amazing team member – just know that we’re headquartered in Lviv, Ukraine!

Career

Content Production Coordinator
Location: Lviv, Ukraine

About the position:

At Fanatee, the Content Team is the heart and soul of our products. As a member of the CodyCross Content Operations team, your work will have a large impact on the players’ experience and will influence users all around the world.

We are looking for a motivated and experienced person who speaks English as their first or second language and focuses on delivering high-quality content on time.

We believe that work is better when well planned, so it is important that you are able to report status and results as needed, as well as follow deadlines and contributes to the continuous improvement of the Content team.

Responsibilities:

  • You will coordinate a team of 50+ highly engaged freelancers located in multiple markets around the world
  • You will apply and develop methods to ensure timeliness and quality of the team deliverables
  • As needed, you will hire and train freelancers
  • You will identify potential production issues and will have the freedom to plan short, medium, and long-term solutions as needed

Required Qualifications:

  • Major in language or communications-related fields
  • You must possess excellent written and verbal English communication skills. You should be able to render complex explanations in a clear and concise way to a team which does not speak English as their first language. If you are also fluent in other languages, it would be a plus
  • You must have proven experience in a similar position in a startup or content agency
  • You must be detail-oriented and able to multitask
  • Your organizational skills must be top notch, and you must possess a process-driven mindset
  • You must work independently and be on track of your tasks and responsibilities
  • Enthusiasm or previous experience with word games is a big plus

What we offer:

  • Competitive remuneration.
  • VR + VT.
  • Health and Dental Insurance.
  • Casual environment and dress code.
  • A space for resting moments, full of snacks, fruits, and drinks.
