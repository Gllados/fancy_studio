about us

Meet the FancyStudio team. We are a dedicated group of gamers, who strive to deliver the best possible gaming experiences. At our core lies a belief that games should be played everywhere and at any time.

The digital world allowed us to fulfill our dream: spend our days surrounded by talented people creating products that allow people around the world to enjoy their days just that tiny bit more!

Sounds like something you might enjoy? We’re constantly searching for our next amazing team member – just know that we’re headquartered in Lviv, Ukraine!

Career

Customer Care Agent (Freelancer)
Location: Lviv, Ukraine

About the position:

We are looking for a Community Hero to join our team as a freelancer and assist our players throughout their gaming experience, guiding them through features and functionalities and recommending solutions to possible issues.

If you’re an excellent communicator, familiar with helpdesk software, native in German and fluent in English (more languages are a plus), we’d love to meet you!

Responsibilities

  • Respond to customer queries in a timely and accurate way.
  • Identify customer needs and help customers use specific features
  • Analyze and report product malfunctions (for example, by testing different scenarios or impersonating users).
  • Keep track of technical issues and useful discussions with customers.
  • Monitor customer complaints on social media and reach out to provide assistance.
  • Share feature requests and effective workarounds with team members.
  • Inform customers about new features and functionalities.
  • Follow up with customers to ensure their technical issues are resolved.
  • Gather customer feedback and share it with our Product team.

Requirements:

  • BSc in Business, Marketing, Information Technology or other relevant fields.
  • Native German and fluent English. Working knowledge of Spanish, Portuguese, Italian, French, Russian or Turkish will be considered a plus.
  • Proactively design, pilot and implement process improvements: done is better than perfect
  • Excellent communication and problem-solving skills.
  • Customer service orientation.
  • Multi-tasking abilities.
  • Excellent organizational and analytical skills.
  • Familiarity with our industry and social media is a plus.
  • Experience using help desk software and remote support tools.
  • Ideally, you have a love for the mobile word and trivia games as well as a general understanding of the gaming industry.
